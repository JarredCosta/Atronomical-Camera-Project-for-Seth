    ASICAP Quick Guide – On Android Phone or Pad

    Step 1. OTG required
    Please make sure your phone or pad supports OTG, you can GOOGLE with keyword – “your phone model” + OTG.

    Step 2. App Download
    Download the app.

    Step 3. Turn on OTG
    Find the menu and turn on OTG, the video below shows how to turn on OTG.

    Step 4. Run the ASICap
    Attach ASI camera or EFW to the OTG cable and run the app.

    Note
    * Some phone cannot provide enough power to power up the camera, then you need to use one hub with external power supply.
